Sensei

Sensei is a tool that allows small business owners to leverage existing CCTV footage to obtain statistical insights about their customer base. The tool works with CCTV cameras focused on doors and observes every time a customer walks in, making note of the time stamp. It also attempts to check if the customer has been to the store before.
